KL UNIVERSITY

About

KL University, officially known as Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KLEF), is a well-regarded private university in India, recognized for its focus on engineering and technology programs. It's known for its strong industry connections, research focus, and commitment to holistic development. The university has two campuses, one in Vijayawada and another in Hyderabad.
